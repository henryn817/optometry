Dr. Kim Nguyen

Dr. Kim Nguyen, OD, is the experienced optometrist and owner of Houston Family Eyecare in Humble, Texas where she puts patient health and outcomes above all else. Dr. Nguyen takes an integrative approach to eye care, partnering with her patients to design a plan that meets their vision needs, utilizing a combination of cutting-edge treatments and alternative therapies.

After earning her Bachelor of Arts in biology from University of Texas, Arlington, Dr. Nguyen went on to earn her Doctor of Optometry from the University of Houston, College of Optometry in Houston, TX.

Prior to opening Houston Family Eyecare, Dr. Nguyen gained valuable experience working for private optometry practices throughout the greater Houston area. Her ultimate goal was to always serve her community and help change the patient perspective on the value of eye care by making it accessible and affordable for all.
